Game Rules:

Back Play!

1. Choose Your Player Name

Enter a name you’d like to use for the game. You can reuse the same name each time you play, and all your game results will be saved under this name.

2. Pick a Product Category

Select a category of products you’re interested in.

3. Read the Product Description

In the beginning of the game, a product description will appear. Take a moment to understand the product before guessing.

4. Guess the Sentiment Score

After reading the description, you’ll be shown a user review. Based on this comment, guess the sentiment score from 1 to 10 (where 1 is extremely negative and 10 is extremely positive).

5. View the Results

Once you submit your guess, you’ll see the median score of all other players' guesses. You’ll also be shown how close your guess is to this score.

6. Continue or Change Products

After each round, you can continue guessing more reviews in the same product category, or you can choose to switch to a new category anytime.

7. End the Game Anytime

If you want to take a break or stop playing, you can exit the game at any time. Your results will be saved automatically.

Enjoy the game, and see how well you can guess the sentiment!
