Game Rules:

Back Play!

1. Discover Your Player Name

Every player starts with a unique and randomly generated name to make your experience more engaging and personalized. Your name will appear in the top-right corner of the page, representing you throughout the game.

2. Pick a Product Category

Select a category of products you’re interested in. You’ll find a wide variety to choose from, such as mattresses, video games, restaurants, and more!

3. Read the Product Description

At the start of each round, a product description will appear. Be sure to read it carefully to understand the details and context of the product. This understanding will influence the accuracy of your guesses. When you're ready, click the "Start Guessing!" button.

4. Guess the "Sentiment Score" of the review

On this page, you’ll be shown a user review. Based on this information, you will need to guess its "sentiment score" or skip the review if it's difficult to assign a score.
*The "sentiment score" represents how likely the reviewer is to recommend this product to others, from 0 to 10, where 0 is not at all likely and 10 is extremely likely.

5. Submit the Result

Once you've given a "sentiment score" to the review, click the "Rate!" button to submit your guess.

6. Continue or Change Products

After each round, which includes 20 reviews, you can continue guessing more reviews in the same product category, or you can choose to switch to a new category anytime.

7. End the Game Anytime

If you want to take a break or stop playing, you can exit the game at any time. Your results will be saved automatically.

Enjoy the game, and see how well you can guess the "sentiment scores"!
